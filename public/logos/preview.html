Logo Concept Exploration

Concept 01
Shattered Hexagon

Three separate shards that together form a complete shape. The center void represents "the key that never exists." Geometric, technical, and represents distributed key generation.

Concept 02
Connected Nodes

Two nodes connected through secure channels. Directly represents the 2-of-2 MPC architecture. Technical and network-inspired.

Concept 03
Divided Shield

Classic security symbol divided into quadrants. Represents protection through distribution. Lab flask accent at bottom nods to research identity.

Concept 04
Crypto Crystal

A prism that splits light into parts—like splitting a key into shards. Faceted, dimensional, sophisticated. Hollow center for the absent key.

Concept 05
Lab Flask

Classic laboratory flask containing digital shards. Blends science and crypto aesthetics. Strong "Labs" identity with technical undertones.

Concept 06
CL Monogram

Stylized "CL" lettermark with a break in the C representing the shard concept. Clean, versatile, and immediately recognizable as a brand mark.

Concept 07
Threshold Lock

A lock split in two, requiring both halves. Directly communicates 2-of-2 threshold security. Dashed lines show the cryptographic protocol connecting parts.

Concept 08
Abstract C

Layered C letterform with a strategic break. Modern, dynamic, premium feel. Works at any size. Technical accent lines add crypto edge.
